How WastenotHub Works

1. Seller Posts

Sellers create an account and list their e-waste items with details and photos. Items await admin approval before listing.

2. Admin Approves

Our admin team reviews each listing for quality and accuracy before making it available to buyers.

3. Buyer Purchases

Buyers browse approved listings and purchase items they need. All transactions are secure.

4. Volunteer Delivers

Our verified volunteers coordinate pickup and delivery, with status updates at every step.
