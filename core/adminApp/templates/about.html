Our Sustainable Journey

Transforming e-waste into opportunities through innovation and community

Our Mission

WastenotHub is revolutionizing e-waste management by creating a circular economy where electronics get second lives. We connect conscientious sellers with eco-aware buyers and passionate volunteers to ensure every device is either responsibly reused or properly recycled.

Our platform prevents thousands of tons of e-waste from ending up in landfills each year, while providing affordable tech solutions to communities and creating green jobs.

How It Works

For Sellers

List your unused electronics for sale or donation with our easy process. We handle verification and connect you with buyers or recycling partners.

For Buyers

Browse certified refurbished electronics at affordable prices. All items come with quality checks and warranty options.

For Volunteers

Join our green army to help with collection drives, awareness campaigns, and community recycling programs.
