How E-WasteNotHub Works

A simple three-step process to make e-waste recycling effortless

1. Post Your E-Waste

Sellers list their e-waste items with details and photos for admin approval.

2. Schedule Pickup

Our verified eco-couriers collect the items at your preferred time.

3. Responsible Recycling

Items are either refurbished for buyers or recycled following environmental standards.

OUR VISION

To create a world where every piece of electronic waste is responsibly managed, transforming environmental challenges into sustainable opportunities for communities worldwide.

OUR MISSION

We empower individuals and businesses to take action against e-waste through an innovative platform that connects sellers, buyers, and eco-couriers. By facilitating responsible recycling and reuse, we’re building a circular economy that benefits both people and the planet.

OUR VALUES

We stand by environmental responsibility, transparency, and inclusivity. We believe in creating real impact through collective action, honest communication, and a commitment to building a cleaner, greener future for all.

The Impact of E-Waste

Understanding the hidden consequences of improper disposal

Environmental Poisoning

  • Lead contaminates water sources
  • Mercury enters food chains
  • 50M+ tons generated annually

Human Toll

  • Respiratory diseases
  • Neurological damage
  • 15M+ workers at risk

Economic Waste

  • $57B in lost materials
  • Gold, silver, copper wasted
  • Only 17% recycled properly
