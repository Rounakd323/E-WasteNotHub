Guidelines for responsible e-waste management with WastenotHub

Our Mission

WastenotHub is revolutionizing e-waste management through an innovative platform connecting sellers, buyers, volunteers, and administrators. Our mission is to create a sustainable circular economy for electronics while ensuring full compliance with environmental regulations.

Circular Economy Vision: We transform e-waste from environmental burden to valuable resource through secure, regulated transactions between verified users across Kolkata and surrounding areas.

1. Scope of Services

Accepted E-Waste

  • Computers & laptops
  • Mobile phones & tablets
  • Televisions & monitors
  • Refrigerators & AC units
  • Printers & scanners
  • Small household appliances

Service Area

Currently serving:

  • Kolkata metropolitan area
  • Howrah, Hooghly, North/South 24 Parganas
  • Expanding to new regions quarterly

Client Eligibility

  • Individuals (18+ years)
  • Registered businesses
  • Educational institutions
  • Government organizations
  • NGOs & non-profits

2. Compliance with Regulations

E-Waste Rules 2022

  • Registered with CPCB (Reg. No. WB-EW-2023-0456)
  • Monthly reporting to State Pollution Board
  • Authorized collection & recycling partners

Environmental Standards

  • ISO 14001 certified processes
  • Hazardous substance monitoring
  • Regular facility audits

Hazardous Materials

  • Lead content < 0.1% by weight
  • Mercury content < 0.1%
  • Cadmium content < 0.01%
  • Full material declaration

Environmental Compensation

  • ₹10,000 - ₹50,000 for minor violations
  • Up to ₹1,00,000 for major non-compliance
  • Appeal process within 30 days

3. Responsibilities

Collection Protocol

  • Pre-sorted e-waste categories
  • ESD-safe handling procedures
  • Secure data destruction
  • Contamination prevention

Record Keeping

  • Digital manifests for all transactions
  • 3-year retention policy
  • CPCB-formatted monthly reports
  • Audit-ready documentation

Disposal Methods

  • 70% minimum recycling rate
  • Authorized TSDF partners
  • Component recovery tracking
  • Certificates of destruction

Worker Safety

  • PPE requirements
  • Annual health checkups
  • Hazard training certification
  • Emergency response plans

4. Storage & Transportation

Storage Facilities

  • Leak-proof containment
  • 24/7 monitored sites
  • Fire suppression systems
  • Spill containment

Transportation

  • GPS-tracked vehicles
  • Hazardous material certified
  • Chain of custody logs
  • Emergency kits onboard

Storage Period

  • Maximum 180 days storage
  • Quarterly inventory checks
  • Priority processing system
  • Regulatory reporting

5. Client Obligations

Handover Process

  • Pre-notification required
  • Completed declaration form
  • Accessible location
  • No mixed waste

Required Information

  • E-waste category
  • Approximate weight
  • Condition report
  • Original purchase year

Compliance

  • No illegal modifications
  • Battery removal (when possible)
  • No hazardous mixing
  • Truthful declarations

6. Liability & Indemnification

Limited Liability: WastenotHub's liability is limited to the fair market value of the e-waste processed. We are not liable for:

  • Pre-existing data on devices
  • Consequential damages
  • Unauthorized modifications
  • Force majeure events

Client Indemnification: Clients agree to indemnify WastenotHub against claims arising from:

  • Misrepresented e-waste
  • Regulatory violations
  • Third-party claims

7. Termination

Termination Clause

  • 30 days written notice
  • Immediate for violations
  • Survival of key clauses
  • Exit audit provisions

Post-Termination

  • 90-day transition period
  • Final reconciliation
  • Data retention policy
  • Return of assets

8. Dispute Resolution

Three-Step Process:

  1. Negotiation: 30-day good faith discussions
  2. Mediation: ICC-certified mediator
  3. Arbitration: Binding under Indian Arbitration Act
